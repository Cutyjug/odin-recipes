Toast

Description

This is the tutorial to make a delicious toast in under 30 secs. This acts as a quick and tasty meal to fill up your tummy if you are very hungry.

Ingredients:

  • A Bread Piece.
  • Optionally some sides like coleslaw if you have the time.
  • Some Oil/Ghee to coat it
  • ketchup or a dipping sauce like chilli sauce and mayonnaise or ranch.
  1. Take the bread piece and slightly coat them with oil or ghee.
  2. First, Take some ketchup/chilli sauce and mix it with mayonnaise to make our dipping sauce.
  3. Then, Cook this in the stove for 20 - 25 secs on high.
  4. Your Simple Toast is ready! optionally eat it with some sides to really complete the meal like coleslaw or some stew or tomato soup
