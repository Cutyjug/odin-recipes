Sandwich

Description

This is the recipe for a versatile sandwich. You choose the toppings, fillings etc by your likings only.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Bread Pieces.
  • Some fillings of your choice like cooked meat, boiled potatoes etc.
  • some veggies like lettuce, onion, tomatoes etc.
  • ketchup or a dipping sauce like chilli sauce and mayonnaise or ranch.
  1. Take the two bread pieces and slightly coat them with oil or ghee.
  2. First, put a little bit of the ketchup/chilli sauce mixed with mayonnaise on the lower bread.
  3. Then, put the veggies on top and bottom breads.
  4. Stuff the fillings inside in the middle of the sandwich, so it is held together by the veggies
  5. Cook this in a microwave or a sandwich maker for 4-5 minutes until the tops and bottoms are slightly charred.
