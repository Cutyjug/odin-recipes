Pizza

Description:

This is a good recipe.

Ingredients:

  • pizza base
  • 2-3 tablespoons pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional Toppings like Pepperoni, Onions etc.

Steps:

  1. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base, leaving a small border around the edges.
  2. Add shredded mozzarella cheese evenly on top of the sauce. Also place the toppings.
  3. Place it in the microwave on high for 3-5 minutes, until the cheese is melted.
  4. Your Pizza is Ready!
